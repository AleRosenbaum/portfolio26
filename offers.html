Structured offer — make an offer + respond (2 screens)

Structured Offers

Making an offer a first-class action, not a chat message

Buyers make an offer with a clear amount. Sellers get explicit accept, decline, and counter actions instead of a free-text message they may never answer.

  • Every offer has a clear state: open, accepted, declined, or countered
  • Sellers can respond in one tap
Counter offer — seller counters, buyer accepts (2 screens)

Counter Offers

Keeping the negotiation moving with counter offers

A counter offer turns a dead end into a next step. Instead of ignoring a low bid, sellers respond with a price they would accept.

  • Negotiation stays inside one structured flow
  • Fewer conversations end without an answer
